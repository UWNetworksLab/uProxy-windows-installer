Frequently asked questions

What do “get access” and “share access” mean?

When you are getting access from a friend, all the web traffic from your browser first travels encrypted to your friend’s computer and from there, to the Internet. Learn more.

Why does uProxy connect to social networks?

uProxy needs a way to see when your friends are online, and it needs to exchange information (IP address, port, and encryption keys) between peers to set up direct connections. Learn more.

Does uProxy collect data about me?

If you do not opt into uProxy’s data reporting, no data is reported about your usage whatsoever. If you do opt in, any usage data is anonymized before it ever leaves your computer, using the same state-of-the-art, privacy-preserving algorithms used in Google Chrome. Learn more.
